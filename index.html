Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need IELTS to apply?

No, many Georgian universities accept international students without IELTS.

Can I work while studying?

Yes, students are allowed to work part-time while studying in Georgia.

How much does it cost per semester?

Fees vary by program but generally range between $1500 to $3000 per semester.
